Professional Background

My film career started back when I was in my masters program in Film Studies in 2014. My very first "cinematic" experience was a very small cut, the digital marketing section of Sony Pictures Entertainment Beijing Office. There I was introduced how Hollywood blockbusters fitted into Chinese language territory and was involved in the whole marketing process of the title "Spectre"(2015) and partially involved in "The Walk"(2015) and "The Angry Birds Movie"(2016). After leaving SPE, I joined Beijing Culture Motion Pictures Entertainment department with my supervisors back at Sony. There I was opened up to more about opportunities and involved in the creation of the highest grossed ever box office hit the "Wolf Warrior 2". Down the road at Beijing Culture, I've sold the in-house productions and was mainly shouldered the creative development business before I left the company. I have to say the journey was very fruitful and shaped my career goals and passion!
